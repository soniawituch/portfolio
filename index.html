  • Impact: Successfully scaled the platform to serve over 8,000 patients and 100+ CBT-specialized therapists across multiple regions, achieved recognition as EIT Health Top 10 European Mental Health Innovator, and created a sustainable model for accessible mental healthcare delivery that reduced barriers to treatment access.

  • therapeutic compatibility matching

  • compatibility scoring
